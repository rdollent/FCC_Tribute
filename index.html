Grace Hopper

Amazing Grace

Timeline of her life:

  • 1906 - Born in New York City
  • 1934 - Earned her PhD in Mathematics from Yale
  • 1944 - Worked as a programming staff on Harvard's Mark I computer
  • 1949 - Became a senior mathematician and worked on the development of UNIVAC I
  • 1959 - Served as a technical consultant to what would become COBOL
  • 1967 - Became the director of the Navy Programming Languages Group
  • 1973 - Promoted to Captain rank
  • 1983 - Promoted to Rear Admiral rank
  • 1992 - Died at age 85
In pioneer days they used oxen for heavy pulling, and when one ox couldn't budge a log, they didn't try to grow a larger ox. We shouldn't be trying for bigger computers, but for more systems of computers.
Grace Hopper, on the building of bigger computers.
